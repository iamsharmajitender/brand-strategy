## 1. Core pillars (Core Domains)

Four domains only â€” same as [jitendersharma.dev](https://jitendersharma.dev). **Not** LLM, RAG, or Cloud as separate pillars; those are **topic tags**.

| Slug | Core pillar | Definition |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `strategy-architecture` | **Strategy & Architecture** | Roadmaps, reference models, and design authority for enterprise transformation. |
| `platforms-engineering` | **Platforms & Engineering** | Cloud-native, event-driven, and observable systems that scale. |
| `ai-intelligence` | **AI & Intelligence** | Governed agents, RAG, and production AI on enterprise foundations. |
| `governance-trust` | **Governance & Trust** | Policy, compliance, and operational resilience in regulated environments. |

**Planning tables** may use the full Core pillar name (e.g. AI & Intelligence). **Site frontmatter** uses the slug as the `domain` tag.
