# Voice Tags

Primary voice is how an **Insight reads** on site. **Insights only** — G.A.I.N · Blueprint · Architecture · Playbook carry **no voice tag**.

**Rule:** exactly **one primary voice** per Insight. The paired **LinkedIn voice post** uses the **same voice** — one post per Insight, shorter format, link back to site.

**Tagging rules:** [Tagging Guidelines](./Tagging-Guidelines-for-Insights) · **Post templates:** [Post Engine](../../post/Overview)

| Slug | Label | Core question | Length | Use when |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `pov` | **POV** | What belief am I challenging? | 150–400 words | Challenging a belief · strong positioning |
| `lrn` | **LRN** | What did I learn in the field? | 200–500 words | Field lesson · what broke or changed |
| `arch` | **ARC** | What is the architecture pattern or flow? | 300–600 words | Architecture flow · layers · design principles |
| `exp` | **EXP** | How do I explain this without losing truth? | 200–400 words | Explaining a complex idea clearly |

**Site frontmatter:** voice slug → `type` tag.

**LinkedIn lengths** apply to the paired voice post, not the Insight on site.

## When to use each voice

**POV** — Contrarian belief. End with a memorable principle.

**LRN** — Field lesson. What broke or changed your thinking.

**ARC** — One architecture flow or layer table — not the full Insight.

**EXP** — Simplified explanation without dumbing it down.
