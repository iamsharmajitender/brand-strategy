### Positioning statement

> I help enterprise leaders design **governed AI-native systems** — not demos. My work sits at the intersection of strategy, platform architecture, and production AI in regulated environments. I build this thinking in public through frameworks, architecture breakdowns, and advisory-ready content.

### What makes you different

Most AI content focuses on models, prompts, and tools. Your angle is **systems architecture**:

- AI is a **governed system**, not an intelligent component
- **Identity, policy, and observability** matter as much as the model
- **G.A.I.N** gives you a repeatable operating model competitors don't have
- You speak from **regulated enterprise experience**, not startup demos

## Brand voice rules

1. **Architectural, not marketing** — problem/tension titles, not hype
2. **Enterprise-scale consequences** — always answer "why this matters at scale"
3. **Systems over models** — redirect from model debates to architecture decisions
4. **One clear idea per voice post** — one voice, one takeaway on LinkedIn
5. **Show the flow** — diagrams and layer tables in Insights
6. **Close with a principle** — one line the reader can repeat
7. **Always link back** — every LinkedIn voice post points to the Insight on your site
